EQUIPMENT On Krupa and Zrmanja, different kind of boats can be used, for bigger comfort and safety. Two persons kayaks are stabile and easy to manage, and we use them during the summer, when water level is low . During the high water levels (autumn, spring), we use raft boats (for 8 to 10 people). Each kayak is equipped with waterproof bag (for dry clothes, food, camera etc.). You will get a helmet and a PFD, which are required. Depending of a weather conditions, we will provide additional equipment for lower temperatures – neoprene suits IMPORTANT! RAFTING BOATS ARE USED ONLY IN CASE OF HIGH WATER LEVEL AND RAFTING SEASON IS USUALLY IN APRIL AND MAY, KAYAK SEASON IS USUALLY FROM JUNE UNTILL NOVEMBER
